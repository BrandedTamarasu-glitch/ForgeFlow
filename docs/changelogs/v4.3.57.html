Forgeflow V4.3.57 · 8 June 2026

Release: Review-Auto Sandbox Policy Contract

Forgeflow 4.3.57 lands Phase 4 of the review-auto sandbox path. The classifier now emits a deterministic policy contract before any sandbox runner exists, so future auto-fix proposals can distinguish safe proposal candidates from risky or blocked findings without editing source files.

Structured Finding Contract

  • /forgeflow-review-auto-classify normalizes class, files, evidence_count, target file, source, tier, and policy metadata.
  • Classifier output includes policy version, matched rules, reasons, proposal eligibility, and sandbox-required status.
  • Saved evidence artifacts render the same policy contract for handoff and review.

Deterministic Policy

  • Allowlisted single-file classes can become future sandbox proposal candidates.
  • Unknown or unapproved classes stay risky.
  • Denylisted classes and surfaces such as auth, permissions, secrets, migrations, dependencies, release publishing, settings files, broad behavior changes, and product judgment are not auto-applicable.

Schema Safety

  • /forgeflow-review-evidence-schema now inspects files[] before classification.
  • Multi-file findings and path hazards are surfaced before classifier or evidence consumption.
  • The classifier remains read-only: no source edits, worker dispatch, commits, pushes, or sandbox runner behavior.
